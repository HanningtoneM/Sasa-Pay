SasaPay is a mobile money e-wallet that offers the easiset way to make payments at the most affordable cost.

Licensed and Regulated by Central Bank of Kenya.

Person to Person

SasaPay allows users to send and receive money at affordable rates.

Pay Merchants and Utilities

With SasaPay Till and Paybill numbers, you can make payments at any point of sale.

Send to other users in other channels

Moving money to and from SasaPay to other mobile money service providers (M-Pesa, T-Kash, AirtelMoney and Banks) has never been this easy.

Escrow Payments

SasaPay provides you with an Escrow account which receives and disburses money for the primary transacting parties, with the disbursement dependent on conditions agreed to by the transacting parties.

Sub-wallets

SasaPay has introduced sub-wallets to enable our users plan their daily earnings as well as set aside money in small amounts to achieve a larger financial goal. SasaPay makes this process for the user safe, simple, efficient, seamless and easy to reconcile for all earnings and expenditures.

Save Loose Change

No sweets. You can get all your loose change as low as KES. 1 loaded into your SasaPay wallet.
